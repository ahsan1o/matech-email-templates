## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Should I customize each email?**
A: YES. Generic emails get deleted. Specific, personal emails get responses.

**Q: How many times should I follow up?**
A: 3 times max. First email → 7 days later → 14 days later. Then move on.

**Q: What if they don't respond?**
A: Move on. They're not a fit. Focus on the next prospect.

**Q: Should I use email automation?**
A: NO. At least not at first. Manual emails get higher response rates. Automate AFTER you have a process.

**Q: What's the best response rate?**
A: 10-20% is good. 20%+ is great. Focus on quality, not quantity.

**Q: Should I be doing this daily?**
A: 30-45 minutes per day is enough. Send when you can. Track everything.
