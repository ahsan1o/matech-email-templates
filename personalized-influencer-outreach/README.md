## How To Send Them

1. Copy the DM block from the `.txt` file and send it first.
2. Wait 24 to 48 hours, then copy the email body and send it as the follow-up.
3. Replace the calendar link in the email if you want a booking CTA.
4. If a creator replies in DM, continue there and skip the email unless they ask for it.
5. If you want maximum speed, use the plain-text `.txt` files and ignore the HTML ones.

## Sending Notes

- Keep the DM under 4 lines if possible.
- Use the email to add the context that would feel too long in DM.
- Do not send all six on the same day if you want replies to feel manageable.
- Track replies in a simple sheet with name, channel, sent date, follow-up date, and status.

## Fastest Copy-Paste Workflow

1. Open the relevant `.txt` file.
2. Copy the `Subject` line into your email subject.
3. Copy the `Hi...` through the signature block into the email body.
4. Copy the `DM:` line into Instagram or WhatsApp DM.
